White Chocolate & Raspberry Sponge Cake

Light sponge cake + raspberry jam + white chocolate ganache.

Click to open
Cake

1) Sponge Cake

Ingredients
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 180¬∞C (normal).
  2. Whip eggs + powdered sugar until very fluffy and thick.
  3. Mix in vanilla.
  4. Sift flour + baking powder, then fold gently into the batter.
  5. Bake until golden and done (toothpick test).
  6. Cool completely before slicing.

2) White Chocolate Ganache

Ingredients
  • 200g white chocolate (chopped)
  • 120g heavy cream
  • Pinch of salt (optional)
Instructions
  1. Heat cream until steaming (don‚Äôt boil).
  2. Pour over white chocolate, wait 2 minutes.
  3. Stir until smooth (add salt if you want it less sweet).
  4. Chill until thick/spreadable (1‚Äì2 hours).
  5. Optional: whip after chilling for a fluffier filling.

3) Raspberry Layer

Ingredients
  • Raspberry jam
  • Optional: fresh raspberries
Instructions
  1. Warm jam slightly so it spreads easily.
  2. Spread a thin layer of jam between cake layers.
  3. Add a few raspberries if you want extra texture.

4) Assembly

Notes
  • Slice sponge into 2‚Äì3 layers.
  • Alternate ganache + jam.
  • Chill before slicing for clean layers.
Steps
  1. Layer 1 ‚Üí ganache ‚Üí jam.
  2. Layer 2 ‚Üí ganache ‚Üí jam.
  3. Top with ganache (and sides if you want).
  4. Chill 30‚Äì60 minutes, then slice.

Tres Leches

Cake sponge + milk mixture + whipped cream.

Click to open
Cake

1) Cake (Sponge)

Ingredients
  • 125g flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • Salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 5 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup sugar
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 180¬∞C.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder & salt.
  3. Beat 3/4 cup sugar + egg yolks until pale; add milk + vanilla.
  4. Gently stir wet into dry (don‚Äôt overmix).
  5. Beat egg whites to stiff peaks; gradually add 1/4 cup sugar.
  6. Fold egg whites into batter gently.
  7. Pour into ungreased pan; smooth top.
  8. Bake 25‚Äì35 mins.

2) Whipped Cream

Ingredients
  • 0.25L whipping cream
  • 3 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions
  1. Whip cream + powdered sugar + vanilla until fluffy.

3) Milk Mixture

Ingredients
  • 355 ml evaporated milk
  • 390 g condensed milk
  • 61 ml whole milk
Instructions
  1. Whisk all 3 milks together.
  2. Poke holes in cooled cake and slowly pour milk mixture over it.
  3. Chill at least 4 hours (best overnight).

Mathilda Chocolate Cake

Cake + ganache (ganache first).

Cake

1) Cake

Ingredients
  • 4 eggs
  • 300g brown sugar
  • 120 ml oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 160 ml milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • 45g cocoa powder
  • 160g all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp baking powder
Instructions
  1. Mix eggs, brown sugar, oil, vanilla.
  2. Add milk + sifted dry ingredients.
  3. Whisk until combined.
  4. Bake at 180¬∞C ~40 mins.

2) Ganache

Ingredients
  • 113g butter
  • 86 ml water
  • 30g (2 tbsp) brown sugar
  • 300‚Äì320g semi-sweet chocolate
  • 40g milk chocolate
  • 250 ml heavy cream
Instructions
  1. Start with ganache.
  2. Heat butter + water + brown sugar until boiling.
  3. Turn off heat, add chocolate, stir.
  4. Stir in heavy cream.
  5. Cover (wrap touching surface). Chill 8 hours.
