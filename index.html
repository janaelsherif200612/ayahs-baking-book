Tres Leches

Cake sponge + milk mixture + whipped cream.

Click to open

1) Cake (Sponge)

Ingredients
  • 125g flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • salt
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1/3 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 5 egg whites
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 180¬∞C
  2. Mix 125g flour, 1.5 tsp baking powder, & salt in a bowl
  3. In another bowl mix 3/4 cup of sugar to 5 egg yolks at high speed until pale yellow. Then add 1/3 cup of milk and 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  4. Pour egg yolk mixture to the flour mixture and stir gently (don't overmix)
  5. In another bowl beat egg whites (5) on high-speed and as it becomes stiff peaks, gradually mix in 1/4 cup of sugar
  6. Pour all of the egg-white mixture into the batter and gently combine with a spatula
  7. Pour batter into an ungreased pan and smooth it into an even layer
  8. Bake for 25‚Äì35 mins

2) Whipped Cream

Ingredients
  • 0.25L whip cream
  • 3 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions
  1. Whip 0.25L cream with powdered sugar and vanilla until fluffy.

3) Milk Mixture

Ingredients
  • 355 ml evaporated milk
  • 390 g condensed milk
  • 61 ml whole milk
Instructions
  1. Whisk all 3 milks together until fully combined.
  2. After cake is baked and slightly cooled, poke holes and slowly pour milk mixture over it.
  3. Chill at least 4 hours (best overnight).

1) Cake

Ingredients
  • 200g butter (for browned butter)
  • 213 g flour
  • 300g sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • salt
  • 170g brown sugar
  • 120 ml sour cream
  • 120 ml whole milk
  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vanilla paste
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 180¬∞C
  2. Browned butter: melt 200g butter over medium heat, then cool it down (stir while it cools in the fridge).
  3. Mix flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, & salt.
  4. In a separate bowl beat brown sugar, sour cream, milk, egg whites, vanilla extract & vanilla paste.
  5. Add wet to dry and mix.
  6. Bake 30‚Äì35 mins.

2) Dulce de leche cream

Ingredients
  • Dulce de leche
  • Sour cream
Instructions
  1. Mix dulce de leche with sour cream.
  2. Mix until smooth and spreadable. Chill 15‚Äì30 minutes if it feels too loose.

1) Carrot Cake

Ingredients
  • 200‚Äì260 g chopped roasted walnuts
  • 300g brown sugar
  • 100g granulated sugar
  • 240 ml oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • optional: 180g smooth unsweetened applesauce
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 313 g all-purpose flour
  • 2.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • ~2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 220‚Äì260 g grated carrots (3‚Äì4 carrots)
Instructions
  1. Toast nuts (5 mins air-fryer max crisp).
  2. Preheat oven to 177¬∞C.
  3. Whisk brown sugar, granulated sugar, oil, eggs, applesauce, & vanilla until combined.
  4. In another bowl whisk flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, ginger.
  5. Pour wet into dry and fold with spatula until combined.
  6. Bake 20‚Äì25 mins.
  7. Make cream cheese frosting.

2) Cream Cheese Frosting

Ingredients
  • cream cheese (softened)
  • butter (softened)
  • powdered sugar
  • vanilla
  • pinch of salt
Instructions
  1. Beat softened cream cheese and softened butter until smooth.
  2. Mix in powdered sugar gradually until thick and spreadable.
  3. Add vanilla + pinch of salt. Chill briefly if needed.

Mathilda Chocolate Cake

Cake + ganache (ganache first).

Click to open

1) Cake

Ingredients
  • 4 eggs
  • 300g brown sugar
  • 120 ml oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 160 ml milk
  • pinch of salt
  • 45g cocoa powder
  • 160g all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp baking powder
Instructions
  1. Mix eggs, brown sugar, oil, and vanilla.
  2. Add milk and sifted dry ingredients (salt, cocoa, flour, baking powder).
  3. Whisk until combined.
  4. Bake at 180¬∞C for ~40 mins.

2) Ganache

Ingredients
  • 113g butter
  • 86 ml water
  • 30g (2 tbsp) brown sugar
  • 300‚Äì320 g semi-sweet chocolate
  • 40g milk chocolate
  • 250 ml heavy cream
Instructions
  1. Start with ganache.
  2. Heat butter, water, and brown sugar until it boils.
  3. Turn off heat, add chopped chocolate, stir until melted.
  4. Stir in heavy cream.
  5. Cover with plastic wrap touching the surface. Chill 8 hours.
